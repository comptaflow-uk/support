üîßInstallation & Activation

How do I retrieve my licence key?

Your licence key is sent automatically by email after purchase (from noreply@meetempo.com). You can also find it by logging in to your customer account: My Account

Can I use my licence on multiple sites?

No, each licence is linked to a single domain name. If you manage multiple sites, you need a separate subscription for each.

What happens if I change WordPress sites?

Contact our support at support@meetempo.com to deactivate the licence on the old site and activate it on the new one. This can be done once per quarter.

üí°Usage

Does ComptaFlow UK by MeeTempo replace an accountant?

ComptaFlow UK by MeeTempo simplifies your bookkeeping and generates all compliant documents, but does not replace a qualified accountant for final review and official submissions. However, it significantly reduces accountancy fees by automating 80% of the work.

Are the exports truly HMRC compliant?

Yes, absolutely. Our exports comply with HMRC record-keeping requirements and Making Tax Digital standards. They use UK nominal codes compatible with Sage, Xero and QuickBooks.

Can I import my previous accounting data?

You can import your bank statements (CSV, OFX) for reconciliation. For full import of accounting entries, contact support who will guide you based on your situation.

How do I export data for my accountant?

Go to ComptaFlow UK by MeeTempo ‚Üí Exports and generate the Trial Balance, General Ledger or Journal. These are standard formats accepted by all UK accountants. You can export in both CSV and PDF formats.

üßæVAT & Returns

How do I prepare my VAT Return with ComptaFlow UK?

PLUS and VIP plans only: Go to ComptaFlow UK by MeeTempo ‚Üí VAT Returns. The return is pre-filled automatically with your data. Verify the amounts, then submit via HMRC's online services or your MTD-compatible software.

Does ComptaFlow UK submit my VAT Return automatically?

No. ComptaFlow UK prepares your returns (automatic calculations, pre-filling) but does not submit them. You must use HMRC's online services or MTD-compatible software to file.

What if I'm not VAT registered?

Select "Not VAT registered" in Settings ‚Üí VAT Scheme. ComptaFlow UK will not apply VAT to your transactions and you won't need to file VAT Returns.

üí≥Billing & Subscription

How do I change plans?

Log in to My Account and click "Manage my subscription". You'll be redirected to the Stripe portal where you can change plans instantly.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Go to My Account ‚Üí "Manage my subscription" ‚Üí "Cancel subscription". You keep access until the end of the paid period.

What happens if my payment fails?

Stripe will automatically retry 3 times over 2 weeks. You'll receive an email alert to update your card. If all attempts fail, your licence will be suspended until payment is resolved.

Can I get a refund?

Contact our support at support@meetempo.com within 14 days of purchase if the plugin doesn't meet your expectations. We comply with the Consumer Contracts Regulations 2013.
