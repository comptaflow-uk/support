UK Invoice Requirements

HMRC requires VAT invoices to include specific information. ComptaFlow UK ensures every invoice meets these requirements:

  • A unique, sequential invoice number
  • Your business name and address
  • Your VAT registration number (if applicable)
  • The client's name and address
  • Invoice date and tax point
  • Description of goods or services supplied
  • The net amount, VAT rate, VAT amount, and gross total
  • Payment terms and due date
All handled automatically. ComptaFlow UK populates your business details from Settings, assigns sequential numbers, calculates VAT, and formats everything in a clean, professional layout.
